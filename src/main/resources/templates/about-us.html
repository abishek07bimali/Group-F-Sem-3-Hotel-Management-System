Know more about us

The Luxury hotel offers the ultimate in comfort, style, and service. Guests can expect to be treated to spacious, elegantly appointed rooms and suites, complete with the latest in modern amenities. The hotel's facilities are designed to cater to the needs of even the most discerning traveler, with features such as a full-service spa, a state-of-the-art fitness center, and multiple dining options that range from casual bistros to fine dining restaurants. The hotel staff is dedicated to providing personalized attention and exceptional service, ensuring that every guest's stay is truly unforgettable. Whether you are planning a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a business trip, a luxury hotel offers the perfect blend of elegance, comfort, and convenience. So, if you're looking for a luxurious and relaxing experience, a luxury hotel is an excellent choice.

2017

The hotel completed its most recent renovation, further enhancing the guest experience with the addition of additional amenities such as a swimming pool and a business center. Throughout its history, Luxary Hotel has remained true to its vision of providing guests with the ultimate luxury experience

2016

The hotel's main restaurant was refurbished and rebranded to offer a more upscale dining experience. The hotel's bar also received a facelift and expanded its menu to include a wider range of cocktails and wines.

2015

The hotel introduced its exclusive Executive Lounge, which was exclusively available to guests staying in executive level rooms. The lounge was designed to provide a comfortable and productive space for business travelers, with amenities such as a private meeting room, complimentary high-speed internet, and a dedicated concierge.

2012

Luxury hotel added a rooftop restaurant and lounge, which offered stunning views of the city. The restaurant served delicious food and cocktails, and became a popular spot for guests to unwind and enjoy a meal.

2011

The hotel added a new spa and fitness center, which quickly became a favorite among guests looking to relax and rejuvenate during their stay. The spa offered a variety of treatments such as massages, facials, and body wraps, while the fitness center was equipped with the latest cardio and strength-training equipment.

2007

Luxary Hotel underwent a major renovation, introducing new guest rooms and upgrading its facilities.
