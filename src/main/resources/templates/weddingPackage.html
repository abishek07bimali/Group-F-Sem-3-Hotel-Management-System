Weddings with Luxury

Hotel Luxury provides an exquisite wedding location offering uncompromising service, facilities, theme party facilities and exquisite cuisine to meet your needs. The wedding facilities at our hotel offer a range of options from beautiful landscape gardens, to the Luxury Ballroom (wedding/marriage hall) and an array of guestrooms, suites and leisure facilities; Luxury Hotel offers choice and variety unsurpassed in Nepal.

At Luxury we are passionate about creating beautiful, stress free events. We’ve done over 400 weddings and know that planning a wedding doesn’t have to be a stressful experience.

Hotel Luxury boasts, having one of the biggest wedding spaces in town. The Luxury Ballroom can accommodate up to 600 guests at a time or alternately it can be sub-divided into two intimate venues. An outdoor pre-function area which opens on to a garden is also available for cocktail receptions. A total of 3,125 sq m of outdoor function space is available within the beautifully landscaped hotel grounds suitable for large scale weddings.
